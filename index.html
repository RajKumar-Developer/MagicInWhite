ibangle


The iBangle, designed by Gopi Prasannaa in 2008, was the first-of-its-kind wearable music device, conceptualized long before Apple introduced wearables.

This futuristic bangle-style iPod featured touch-sensitive controls, virtual earphones, and an adjustable fit, redefining personal audio experiences. Recognised globally, it was featured in CNN's "21st Century Visionary" list and highlighted by Yanko Design as one of the Best of 2008, showcasing its pioneering approach to wearable technology.
